Earlier working experiences:

Key Music

2015 was the year that I wanted to start my own shop. A retailshop in musical instruments was the goal. Soon after the start we were added to the franchise plan of Key Music.
We sold anything that could make noise, from guitars to dj-sets and everything in between. I had been a drummer in a rockband for quite a while and this shop was a dream come true.
Sadly, after every dream we wake up. In this case that meant dealing with diminishing margins that led to a bankruptcy.

I ran this store from A-Z. This meant: Accounting, HR, scheduling, buying and selling and much more...
